Dear colleagues,

I am writing to inform you of an upcoming change to our Brightcove video reporting capabilities. As part of our ongoing platform consolidation and in line with current resource priorities, **the MIDI-based Brightcove reporting service will be decommissioned by end of Q2 2026 (30 June 2026).**

## Why are we making this change?

The MIDI platform, which currently provides consolidated Brightcove video analytics, has reached end-of-life due to mandatory security updates. Continued operation and maintenance of the platform is no longer a viable option under current resource constraints and organizational priorities. This decision has been made following a thorough review at leadership level.

## What does this mean for you?

Effective 30 June 2026, the following will no longer be available:

- Consolidated cross-channel Brightcove reporting through MIDI
- Custom report generation by the Internal Comms Analytics team
- Single-view dashboards spanning multiple distribution channels

**Brightcove's native self-service reporting remains fully available** and provides all key metrics including views, engagement rates, and completion data. To access these reports, you will need to request the appropriate BBS access rights.

## Action required: Request BBS access

To ensure a seamless transition, please complete the following steps **by mid-May 2026**:

1. **Identify the Brightcove channels** you currently use for reporting (e.g., Internet, Intranet, or other distribution channels).
2. **Submit a BBS access request** for each channel you require. Please note: a separate request is needed per channel. A step-by-step guide is attached to this email (see PDF attachment).
3. **Obtain line manager approval** for each request. Access will be provisioned within the standard SLA.
4. **Log into Brightcove Analytics** once your access is confirmed to familiarise yourself with the native reporting interface.

> *Please note: Unlike the MIDI platform, Brightcove's native reporting does not offer a single consolidated view across all channels. Each channel must be accessed and reviewed individually. We understand this is less convenient and appreciate your understanding as we align with the organisation's broader platform strategy.*
